# Preview AI - Virtual Try-On for WooCommerce

Preview AI is a powerful WooCommerce plugin that allows your customers to preview products (especially clothing) in real-time using advanced AI image generation. Enhance your store's user experience and reduce returns by letting users see how they would look with your products.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **AI-Powered Virtual Try-On:** High-quality generation of users wearing your clothing items.
* **WooCommerce Integration:** Works out of the box with your existing product catalog.
* **Elementor Ready:** Includes a dedicated widget for easy placement on custom product pages.
* **SaaS Integration:** Fast processing handled by our dedicated AI backend (api.previewai.app).
* **Multilingual & Translation Ready:** Fully compatible with WPML, Loco Translate, and standard WordPress i18n practices.
* **Broad Theme Compatibility:** Designed to work seamlessly with popular themes like Botiga, Astra, OceanWP, and more.

## 🛠 Installation

1. Upload the `preview-ai` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Follow the onboarding wizard to activate your free trial and analyze your product catalog.

## 📖 Technical Overview

This plugin acts as a bridge between your WordPress site and the **Preview AI SaaS platform**. 
- **API Communication:** Securely connects to `https://api.previewai.app/api/`.
- **Image Processing:** Image analysis and generation are performed on our specialized AI infrastructure to ensure high performance without taxing your WordPress server.
- **Privacy:** User photos are processed for generation and are not stored permanently.
